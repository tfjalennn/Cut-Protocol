Why We Built This

Cut Protocol started in 2021 when our founder — a longtime amateur wrestler turned BJJ competitor — couldn't find a single brand that spoke directly to the weight cut experience. Every product was either too generic, too expensive, or not designed with combat sports in mind.

So we built it ourselves. We partnered with fighters across the UFC, Bellator, ADCC, and the amateur wrestling circuit to develop and test every single product in our lineup under real cut conditions.

Our mission is simple: give fighters the best possible tools for the safest, most effective weight cut. Because when you step on that scale, there's no room for gear that lets you down.

Our Promise

Every product in our catalog is combat-sport tested. We don't sell anything we wouldn't put in our own bag the night before weigh-ins. That's the Cut Protocol guarantee.
